What We Offer

Our Medical Services

From prevention to treatment, our specialists cover a broad spectrum of health needs under one roof.

General Medicine

Routine health check-ups, chronic disease management, and preventive care for all ages.

Pediatrics

Specialized healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents with compassionate, family-centered care.

Cardiology

Advanced cardiac diagnostics and treatment, including ECG, echocardiography, and risk management.

Gynecology

Comprehensive women's health services including prenatal care, gynecological exams, and family planning.

Orthopedics

Diagnosis and treatment of bone, joint, and muscle conditions with non-surgical and surgical options.

Neurology

Expert care for conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system.

Ophthalmology

Full-spectrum eye care from routine vision tests to treatment of complex eye diseases.

Pathology & Lab

Comprehensive on-site laboratory testing with rapid, accurate results for faster diagnosis.
